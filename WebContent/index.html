UB Book Bidding System

University of Bridgeport is introducing a new system where members of UB can auction their books and other materials for educational purposes. This new facility is brought about in light of reducing the financial burden on the students, and at the same time to help the help the students earn some money. The auction is designed such that the the seller decides who wins the auction. Each College under UB has an admin who oversees and facilitates the auction. Any member of the UB community can participate in the auction as a seller or a bidder.
